Join COSINE Lab

The COmputational Signal INformatics and Exploration (COSINE) Lab develops cutting-edge AI and signal-processing methods to decode and understand complex biomedical and natural signals. Our research spans brain–computer interfaces, human–machine interfaces, automated sleep analysis, clinical EEG analysis, and pathological speech processing. We aim to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence research and real-world healthcare impact through collaborative science and open innovation. Before writing to us, please explore our Publications and Research pages to understand our scope and expectations.

To apply: email ayush.tripathi@iiti.ac.in with the subject line Application - [Role] - [Your Name] describing your interests and your CV.
