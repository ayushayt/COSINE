Research Areas

The COmputational Signal INformatics and Exploration (COSINE) Lab develops cutting-edge AI and signal-processing methods to decode and understand complex biomedical and natural signals. Our research spans brain–computer interfaces, human–machine interfaces, automated sleep analysis, clinical EEG analysis, and pathological speech processing. We aim to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence research and real-world healthcare impact through collaborative science and open innovation.
